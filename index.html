About

I am a third-year undergraduate student major in biomedical engineering at South China University of Technology with a profound passion for applying AI method to medical research, aspiring to make contributions to human health. Currently, I am working as a visiting scholar in Southern University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof.Quanying Liu, where I focus on parameter-efficient personalized adaptation fine-tuning method for brain foundation model. Before, I am working under the supervision of Prof.Yanwu Xu and Prof.Lin Shu.

My primary research interest are AI4Neuroscience, Brain-computer Interface, Deep Learning and Brain-inspired AI. Always eager to expand my research experience, I am seeking opportunities for summer research internships in 2026. My long-term goal is to pursue a Ph.D in the near future. If you are interested in chatting with me, feel free to drop me an email.
